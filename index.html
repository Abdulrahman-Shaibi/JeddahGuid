About Jeddah

Jeddah is a huge city that sprawls along the coast of the Red Sea, connected together by the Corniche, a seaside avenue full of bizarre sculptures and nearly 30 km long.

The old city or al-Balad, on the southern side of modern Jeddah, is a crumbling but fascinating warren of multi-story houses made from coral.

The main thoroughfare Medina Road starts from the northern side of al-Balad and runs all the way to the city.

Best place’s in jeddah city :

1- Al-Balad (Old Town)

You will find yourself in another world and entirely, surrounded by people form all over the arab world, Asia and Africa. Here you will see many forms of dress that are colorful as well as the traditional black. Many of the shops and stores are cut into the sides of buildings and offer stacks of colorful clothing.

2- Al Shallal Theme Park

family-park that features thrill rides, gentle rides for kids, boat rides, arcade games, and a skating rink. There are also many shops and food stalls around the park as well as two full-service restaurants. The park opens every day at five o’clock in the evening and stays open late into the night.

3- FAKIEH AQUARIUM

Tourists of all ages will enjoy a trip to Fakieh Aquarium, Saudi Arabia’s only public aquarium. Visitors can also enjoy a live dolphin show or even the opportunity to swim with the friendly dolphins ,after the aquarium tour, pick up a fun souvenir at one of the many retail shops then enjoy fresh seafood at Blue Ocean Restaurant.

4- KING FAHD’S FOUNTAIN

One of the most popular tourist attractions in Jeddah is King Fahd’s Fountain, the tallest fountain of its kind in the world. it is best appreciated at night when it is illuminated by hundreds of spotlights. This unique fountain is one of the top modern things to see in Jeddah.

5- SAUDI SAFARI (Desert Camp)

enjoy desert safari, it is one of the most important tours that one must have to do in jeddah, and after you can relax and have a saudi Arabian tea and coffee, enjoy a range of activities, including a camel ride, sand boarding, and more.

6- The New Jeddah Waterfront beach

ew Jeddah Waterfront is just an amazing place to be for the locals and visitors especially in winter. whether you want to sit and enjoy the scenery, swim, take your children to play, jog, or cycle in the new track.

World-class shopping

Jeddah has every kind of shopping experience available, from outlet malls to antique shops and handmade craft stores.

when you Take a stroll through the unique shops on Tahlia Street,it contains many upscale department shops, and boutiques, such as Prada, Gucci, and more.

As the Champs-Élysées is for Paris, Tahlia Street is believed to be the heart of Jeddah and Saudi Arabia's wealthiest district.

1- Boulevard

Boulevard is the premium luxury destination of choice in Jeddah that houses the finest selection of many high end renowned international brands.

2- Al Tahlia Street Shops

Large department stores, and malls like the Tahlia Mall and the Serafi Mega Mall. Branches of Danube supermarkets and bakeries also are located in the area, so you can do your grocery shopping, too.

3- Red sea mall

Red Sea Mall is a massive shopping complex that houses over 240,000 square meters of shopping, dining, entertainment space, and office space, plus the five-star Elaf Jeddah Hotel. One of the Red Sea Mall’s biggest draws is the fact that it is home to Jeddah’s first movie theater.

4- Mall of Arabia

Mall of Arabia its has alot of games for kids such as KidZania, a children’s entertainment center, makes it a great destination for families.

5- C-hub Cafe

jeddah city offers so much variety of coffe shops, and c-hub is one of my best local cafe because it has outdoor sitting area with a relaxing atmosphere and an amazing late night view .

6- Jeddah Comedy Club

Al Comedy club is the home of saudi comedy. Offering great stand up, sketch and improv comedy. and surly worth to visit.

Best Foods

One of the greatest things about jeddah is the diversity of the people And the best way to experience jeddah City is by digging into its food!.

Jeddah brings a lot to the table when it comes to food from local tradition dishes to pizza, burgers, bagels and more.

here are the iconic food lists, you have to try while you are in jeddah city.

1- Al Baik

Al Baik restaurant is a must. This major Saudi Arabian fast-food ,it serves a variety of fried chicken and seafood dishes along with crispy fries and their signature sauces. Al Baik is worth a visit. be sure to put Al Baik on your list when you are in jeddah.

2- Knead Bakery

one of the best local breakfast palce to go in the morning thanks to its relaxed vibe and pleasing menu, they have a variety of foods from traditional flafel to Western dishes pancakes.

3- Signature Juices

There's not much better for the body than a blended juice made with the freshest ingredients. And thankfully jeddah is a city which offers a rich wealth of options when it comes to getting your juice, and signature juice is one of the best in town.

4- Section-B

Hamburgers is the most popular food around the world , Section-b is a localy resturant that serve gourmetburgers it’s one of the best choices for Burger in Jeddah. the burger is perfect with fresh ingredients. Prices are in average range

5- Masoub

Masoub is a arabian desert , it’s popular throughout saudi arabia, often served as a breakfast dish. there is several place that serve it, which is banema restaurant, Mathaq Zaman, masoub al gadery.

6- Al khayal Resturant

Al khayal resturant is considered one of the town's best food attractions, Bringing the authentic cuisines. it’s classy turkish restaurant with well set up menu
